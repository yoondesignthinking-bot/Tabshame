What you actually downloaded

Wait, what is this?

TabShame reads your open tabs and diagnoses you with one of 34 archetypes. It's like a horoscope, except backed by data and slightly meaner.

All of it happens locally on your device. We have no server. We genuinely cannot see your tabs from where we are.

We just wrote a friendly assassin to roast you on your own laptop.

Privacy, plainly

We see nothing. Promise.

  • No server. No analytics. No email collection. No cloud sync. Nothing.
  • The only thing that ever leaves your machine is a share card you choose to download. Even then, we never see it.
  • We literally cannot betray you. We don't have the infrastructure for it.

Heads up

Two prompts you might see.

On the new tab page, we'll ask if you want speed-dial tiles for:

  • Recent sites — Chrome's own most-visited list.
  • Bookmark Bar — read-only. We never touch a bookmark.

Both are off by default. Skip them if you want a clean page.
