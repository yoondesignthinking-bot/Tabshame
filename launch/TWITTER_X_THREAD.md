## Reply strategy

- **Within 10 minutes of posting:** reply to any quote-tweet with
  genuine engagement, not "thanks!". E.g. "Yeah The Pre-Date Detective
  is real — was tempted to make it match-day-only but figured worth
  catching on weekday wedding plus-one googling too".
- **Within 30 minutes:** post tweet 6 again as a fresh tweet (not
  threaded) with the install link. Algorithm boosts mid-thread CTAs
  when they get repeated as standalone tweets.
- **Within 2 hours:** if any tweet gets >50 likes, quote-RT it from a
  different angle. "Wasn't expecting this to land — adding 5 archetypes
  this week based on what people DM'd me".

---

## What to avoid

- **No "Day 1 of building TabShame"** — that thread genre is exhausted.
- **No "Let me know what you think"** — sounds insecure and reduces
  reply rate.
- **No engagement bait** ("Drop a 🛍️ if you're The Comparison Shopper").
  Twitter's algo demotes obvious bait.
- **No follower-only links.** The install link should be public and
  in the same thread as the hook.
